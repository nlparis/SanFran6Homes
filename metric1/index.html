Metric 1: Price Per Person, Mapped

  • Using plot.ly, I created a map that shows the price per person.
  • Price per person data calculated using data from listings.csv.
  • Basically you're looking at the price/accomodations where the more red places are more expensive.
  • What was interesting to me is that Airbnb's near the coast are not more expensive.
  • My guess is that the more expensive place are at higher elevations (better view.)
